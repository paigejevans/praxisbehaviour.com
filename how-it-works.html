How it works

From your data
to a live platform.
In weeks, not months.

Praxis is a custom build, not a generic tool. We take your specific data, structure it, and build an intelligence layer on top of it. You do not configure anything. You do not need a technical team. You get a platform that is ready to use from day one.

The process

Four steps from
first call to live platform.

01
Week 1

Discovery

A 30-minute call to understand what data you have, what decisions you are trying to make, and what questions your team asks most often. You do not need to have everything organised. We work with what exists.

What we cover

  • What data assets you currently have
  • Which decisions are hardest to make right now
  • Who on your team will use the platform
  • What a successful platform looks like for you
02
Weeks 1 to 2

Data intake
and structuring

We take everything you share, clean it, and structure it into a unified knowledge base. We also embed your organisational context so the system knows who your audiences are and what your team already knows. This is what makes Praxis specific to you.

We can work with

  • Survey data and crosstabs
  • Focus group transcripts
  • Sales and CRM exports
  • Campaign and media performance data
  • Segmentation studies and brand trackers
  • Internal reports and strategic documents
03
Weeks 2 to 4

Platform build
and calibration

We build the query interface, cross-dataset reasoning engine, behavioural science layer, and anti-hallucination guardrails on top of your structured data. We then test against real questions your team would ask and calibrate until outputs are accurate and useful. You review before it goes live.

What gets built

  • Plain-language query interface
  • Cross-dataset intelligence engine
  • Behavioural science framework layer
  • Anti-hallucination guardrails
  • Source citation system
  • Interactive dashboards and filters
04
Ongoing

Handover
and live access

We walk your team through the platform in a live session and hand it over ready to use. No technical setup, no onboarding programme. Anyone who can type a question can use it from day one. As your data grows, the platform grows with it.

After handover

  • Live platform access for your full team
  • Monthly subscription covers hosting and maintenance
  • New data sources can be added at any time
  • Platform updates included
  • Direct support from Praxis Behaviour.
